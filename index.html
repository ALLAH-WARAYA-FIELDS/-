Our Story

Seven decades of farming excellence in Hed Bagh

👨‍🌾 Family Owned & Operated

Rooted in Hed Bagh

For over 70 years, ALLAH WARAYA FIELDS has been a cornerstone of agricultural excellence in Hed Bagh. What began as a small family venture generations ago has grown into a trusted name for premium wheat, vibrant mustard, and quality rice.

Our 15-acre farm represents more than just land--it's our family's legacy spanning three generations. We combine decades of traditional farming wisdom with sustainable practices to produce crops that meet the highest standards of quality and taste.
